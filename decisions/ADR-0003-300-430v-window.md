# ADR-0003: 300–430 V Operating Window

## Status
Proposed

## Context
Bosch SMG180 OHW reference motor data points to a 400 V-class system with an upper voltage limit around 425–430 V depending source/version.

## Decision
Design the initial battery search around a 300–430 V operating window.

## Consequences
- 120S LFP packs may be acceptable if top voltage is limited to the inverter-safe maximum.
- 96S and 108S LFP packs are voltage-safe but require higher current at the same power.
- 144S LFP packs are rejected for this reference system because their normal voltage is too high.
