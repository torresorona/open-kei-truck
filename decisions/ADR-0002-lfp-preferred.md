# ADR-0002: LFP Preferred Chemistry

## Status
Proposed

## Context
Community preference has been expressed for LiFePO4 / LFP due to durability, cycle life, and perceived safety.

## Decision
Use LFP as the preferred reference chemistry when a technically viable pack exists.

## Consequences
- AMPHERR LFP-400-052 and custom CTS LFP options should be prioritized.
- ABS T350V-50 remains shortlisted but chemistry must be verified.
- Hypercraft remains a strong alternate but is NMC, not the chemistry-preferred path.
