# Supplier Verification Questions

## Universal supplier questions

1. Can a single unit be purchased? What is MOQ?
2. What is the delivered price to the United States?
3. What is lead time?
4. What is exact chemistry?
5. What are exact dimensions and weight?
6. What voltage range is allowed?
7. Can charger/BMS top voltage be limited?
8. What is continuous discharge power/current?
9. What is peak discharge power/current and duration?
10. What derating curves are available for temperature, SoC, and coolant flow?
11. Is BMS included?
12. Are contactors included?
13. Is precharge included?
14. Is main fuse included?
15. Is isolation monitoring included?
16. Is HVIL supported?
17. Is a manual service disconnect included?
18. What CAN messages or DBC files are available?
19. What charger interface is supported?
20. What inverter/VCU interface is supported?
21. What CAD files are available?
22. What certifications are available and can proof be provided?
23. What warranty applies to prototype/open-source/DIY installations?
